### 🇬🇧 English
**Project Overview:**
I designed a versatile AI Conversational Engine capable of being tailored to any business scenario. The system combines Natural Language Processing with powerful automation to solve real-world problems.

**The Demo Video:**
The video shows **one specific application**: an AI Personal Shopper for a Wine Store. It understands user preferences, recommends the perfect bottle (e.g., Barolo), and offers discounts to close the sale.

**Infinite Applications:**
The same underlying architecture can be easily adapted for:
* **Customer Success & Support:** 24/7 automated assistant that handles FAQs and resolves tickets instantly.
* **AI Automation Specialist:** Lead generation pipeline that captures customer data and syncs it with Airtable/CRM via Make.com or Zapier.
* **HR & Business Ops:** Managing candidate inquiries, filtering applications, or answering internal documentation questions.
